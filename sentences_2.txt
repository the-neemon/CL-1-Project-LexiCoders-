**Backpackers India**

**Moderator:** Welcome all new members! Yeh group specifically budget travel ke enthusiasts ke liye hai. Monthly ek destination discuss karenge, March mein Northeast pe focus kar rahe hain.

**Akash:** Guys, Meghalaya ka best time kab hota hai visit karne ka? June mein plan kar raha hoon but worried about monsoons.

**TravelExpert:** @Akash October-November best rehta hai. Monsoon mein waterfalls spectacular hote hain but landslides ka risk rehta hai aur roads close ho jate hain frequently. Time hai toh autumn plan karo.

**Neha:** Shillong se Cherrapunji kitna door hai? Public transport available hai kya? Budget trip plan kar rahi hoon so taxi afford nahi kar sakti daily.

**MountainLover:** @Neha around 50km hai. Shared taxis milte hain Shillong se, 80-100 rupees per person. Early morning nikalna, last return 4PM ke around hota hai.

**Dhruv:** Homestays recommend karoge kya? Hotels se sasta padega?

**BackpackerGuru:** @Dhruv definitely homestays are better! Local culture experience hoga, home-cooked food milega, and yes 30-40% cheaper than hotels. Mawlynnong village mein maine 800/night mein amazing homestay kiya tha last year.

**Priya:** Solo female traveler hoon, Northeast safe hai kya akele travel karne ke liye? First time ja rahi hoon.

**TravelExpert:** @Priya Northeast is one of the safest regions for solo female travelers in India! Maine khud 3 baar solo trips kiye hain. Basic precautions follow karo - late night travel avoid karo, local contacts save karke rakho. People are extremely helpful and respectful.

**Akash:** Itinerary suggestions 7 days ke liye? Must-visit places kaunse hain?

**BackpackerGuru:** @Akash Quick plan:
Day 1-2: Shillong (Police Bazaar, Ward's Lake)
Day 3-4: Cherrapunji (Double Decker Root Bridge, Seven Sisters Falls)
Day 5: Mawlynnong (Asia's cleanest village)
Day 6-7: Dawki (crystal clear river, boat rides)

Budget approx 15K including stay, food and local travel if you're careful with expenses. Meghalaya main mostly vegetarian food kam milta hai, non-veg eaters ke liye paradise hai!
